Background

Hi, my name is Guillaume Cornille , a self-taught web developer who lives in Grenoble in the middle of the mountains.

After many years in sales who taught me a lot of social skills, I decided to run after another challenge, becoming a front-end web developer.
Why? Because since I was a child I always liked built things useful and learn everything I can to get better at it.

So, I decided to start the Hacking Project bootcamp in 2019 where I learn a lot on a really short amount of time! It was a really exciting experience, but really stressful too. After 3 months of hard work, 12 hours a day, even in the week-end, I realized that I really enjoyed building useful thing and I want to continue it so hard that I decided to become a professional front end web developer.
Becoming a web developer will give me the opportunity to create from nothing, every idea that can make user's life better thanks thanks to what I will build.

For that I needed to have a portfolio, this is the v1 and it will become better as far as i get better at it, I hope you'll like it and I'm listening every advice that you have for me to become a better developer.
